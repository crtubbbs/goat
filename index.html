Who is the Goat

For the past few years there has been a big argument in between fans in the NBA community. Many people have different views about who the greatest player of all time is and why they are. The main people that come up are Michael Jordan, Lebron James, and Kobe Bryant. In this website I will give my own opinion and stats from the main contenders so you can make one for yourself as well. I have been an NBA fan my whole life and have studied it as well as have had many debates with others that are interested in the NBA. As you will find there are many different factors that you need to consider before you can truly pick who the best of all time is.
