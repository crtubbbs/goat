Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is most often seen as the goat of the NBA. Some go as far as saying that he is the greatest athlete of all time. He played for 15 seasons and even took a break in the middle of his career to pursure one of his passions in baseball. While in the NBA his career averages were 30.1pts 6.2rbs and 5.3asts.

He went to the finals six times and won each time as well as he won finals MVP each time. Michael had 5 seperate regular season MVPS to add to it as well as a defensive player of the year award. Through his 15 year career he made 14 allstar games. Michael has the stats awards and mindset to be considered the greatest of all time.
